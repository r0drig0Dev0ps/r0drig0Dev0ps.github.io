Features

Everything You Need to Wake Up

Designed to make mornings productive from the very first second.

Math Challenges

Random addition, subtraction and multiplication problems that force your brain to engage before dismissing the alarm.

Difficulty Levels

Choose from Easy, Medium, or Hard. Scale the challenge to match your morning brain power — or lack thereof.

Persistent Alarm

The alarm keeps ringing until you solve the problem correctly. No cheating, no shortcuts, no snoozing.

Custom Sounds

Pick your alarm sound from the system sound library. Wake up to whatever motivates you most.

Multiple Alarms

Set as many alarms as you need. Each one stored securely in a local Room database.

Modern UI

Clean Material Design interface that's intuitive and beautiful. Built following Android best practices.

How It Works

Simple, Yet Effective

Three steps to never oversleep again.

01

Set Your Alarm

Choose your wake-up time, select difficulty level, and pick your preferred alarm sound.

02

Alarm Rings

When it's time, a math problem appears on screen. The alarm won't stop until you solve it!

03

Solve & Rise

Enter the correct answer to dismiss. Your brain is now awake and ready to conquer the day!
